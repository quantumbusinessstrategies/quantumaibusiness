# Quantum AI Business Advertising Launch Plan

## Launch gate

Do not activate paid ads until:

- `https://quantumaibusiness.com` returns the Quantum AI Business site instead of GitHub Pages 404.
- Stripe Payment Links are connected to the four offer buttons.
- Google tag or Meta pixel is connected through repository variables.
- Daily ad budget and target geography are approved by the owner.

## Funnel

1. Ad click lands on `https://quantumaibusiness.com/?utm_source={{platform}}&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=weakness_scan`.
2. Visitor completes the Business Weakness Intake.
3. Site fires a lead conversion event when the readout is generated.
4. Visitor chooses one of four offer paths:
   - Business Weakness Scan
   - Done-For-You Overhaul
   - Lifetime Insight Vault
   - Full-Spectrum Growth

## Campaign 1: Business Weakness Scan

- Objective: Lead generation or sales, depending on Stripe readiness.
- Audience: Small business owners, consultants, agencies, local service businesses, solo operators.
- Hook: "Find the hidden leaks in your business before they drain another month."
- Primary text: "Quantum AI Business scans lead capture, operations, advertising, accounting, and automation readiness. Get a fast readout, then choose a paid path to fix the gaps."
- CTA: "Get Scan"

## Campaign 2: Automation Overhaul

- Objective: Lead generation.
- Audience: Owners already spending on software, ads, CRM, automations, or business coaching.
- Hook: "Your business does not need more random tools. It needs one connected operating system."
- Primary text: "We map the bottlenecks, connect the automations, and build the growth workflow around your business."
- CTA: "Apply Now"

## Campaign 3: Lifetime Insight Vault

- Objective: Sales.
- Audience: DIY founders, side-hustle operators, consultants, creators, and early-stage service businesses.
- Hook: "Keep the AI business-growth perspective on your side permanently."
- Primary text: "Unlock business growth playbooks, automation perspectives, and decision frameworks designed to help you diagnose and improve your company."
- CTA: "Unlock Insight"

## Minimum first test budget

- Google Search: $10-$25/day on intent terms after conversion tracking is active.
- Meta: $10-$25/day on broad owner/founder audiences after pixel is active.
- Review after 3-5 days or 300+ landing page visits, whichever comes first.

## Safety rules

- Do not claim guaranteed profits.
- Do not imply instant, passive, or risk-free income.
- Keep ad copy focused on diagnostics, systems, automation, and business improvement.
- Review all ad-platform policy warnings before publishing.
