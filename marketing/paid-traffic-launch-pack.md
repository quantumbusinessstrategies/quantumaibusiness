## Kill rules

Stop or revise an ad if:

- Spend reaches `$10` with no landing page views.
- Spend reaches `$15` with no package clicks, lead events, or checkout starts.
- Link click cost is high and no one reaches a tracked page event.
- Meta flags the copy as financial, misleading, or restricted.
- The page gets traffic but no `Lead` or `ViewContent` events.

Scale slowly if:

- Purchase happens.
- InitiateCheckout appears more than once.
- Leads include actual business names or URLs.
- Cost per useful signal is under `$5-$10`.

## Daily owner loop

1. Check Stripe for payments.
2. Check Gmail for QuantumAiBusiness logs.
3. Check Meta Events Manager for `ViewContent`, `InitiateCheckout`, and `Purchase`.
4. Check Google Analytics for traffic source and landing pages.
5. Keep the winning hook, pause the weak one.
6. Route all high-ticket or uncertain clients through owner review.

- Do not spam comments, DMs, groups, or forums.

- Do not auto-spend beyond the approved cap.
- Keep public claims focused on diagnostics, routing, strategy, automation, and opportunity.
