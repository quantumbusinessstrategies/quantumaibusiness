Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026.

QuantumAiBusiness collects information submitted through forms, checkout intake, referrals, analytics events, and owner automation logs. This may include business name, website, email, objectives, tools, constraints, traffic source, package interest, and payment status metadata.

Use of Information

Information is used to provide diagnostics, route fulfillment, send owner notifications, process payments, generate reports, improve the site, track campaign performance, and respond to client requests.

Services Used

The site may use Stripe, Vercel, Resend, Google Analytics/Tag, Meta Pixel, Google Apps Script/Sheets, OpenAI, Buffer, and similar operational tools.

Payment Data

Payment card details are handled by Stripe. QuantumAiBusiness receives payment status and related metadata, not full card numbers.
